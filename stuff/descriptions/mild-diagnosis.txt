<h3>You have Mild Acne!</h3>
<h6>Mild Acne is characterized by...</h6>
<ul>
<li>Occasional Breakouts of blackheads (open pores), whiteheads (closed pores), and papules (small pimples of bumps)</li>
<li>Occuring in isolated parts of the face, such as the chin, nose or forehead</li>
<li>Worsening over time if untreated</li>

</ul>
