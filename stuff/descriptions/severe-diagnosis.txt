<h6>Severe acne is characterized by...</h6>
<ul>
    <li>Many breakouts across multiple body parts such as the face, chest, and back.</li>
    <li>Being unaffected by over the counter treatments.</li>

    <li>Extending deep into the skin</li>
    <li>Many occurences of cysts and nodules</li>
    <li>Being noticeably painful.</li>
    <li>Acne Scars</li>
