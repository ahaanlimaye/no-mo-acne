<h3>You have Moderate Acne:</h3>
<p>Don't worry, this is really common, most people eventually have it go away as they get older.</p>
<h6>Moderate Acne is usually characterized by...</h6>
<ul>
<li>Possible all over the body</li>
<li>Red, inflamed, potentially painful</li>
<li>Papules, postules, nodules</li>
<li>Comedones</li>
</ul>
