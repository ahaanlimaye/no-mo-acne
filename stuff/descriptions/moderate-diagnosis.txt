<h3>You have Moderate Acne.</h3>
<h6>Moderate Acne is usually characterized by...</h6>
<ul>
<li>More obvious looking compared to Mild Acne</li>
<li>Possibly being located across the body</li>
<li>Very noticable breakouts</li>
<li>Having a few papules (inflamed blemish) and pastules (inflamed skin pore)</li>
<li>Not being limited to the face</li>
</ul>
