<h3>Treatment for severe acne can be tough.</h3>
<h6>You should immediately consider the following...</h6>
<ul>
    <li>See dermatologist immediately (best treatment for the long run)</li>
    <li>Topical and oral antibiotics</li>
    <li>Injecting corticosteroids (see dermatologist first).</li>
    <li>Incision and drainage (see dermatologist first).</li>
</ul>
